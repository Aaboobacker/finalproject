Vint Cerf

Vint Cerf is an American Internt pioneer, who is recognized as one of the "Fathers of Internet", sharing his title with Bob Kahn.

Robert Elliot Kahn

Robert Elliot "Bob" Kahn,is an electrical engineer, who was the co-inventer of the Internet. Vint Cerf and Bob together invented the TCP/IP.
