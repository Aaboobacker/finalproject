Innovations

ARPA

In 1957, Soivet Union launched sputnik. In additon, President Eisenhower created bureau at the defense department ARPA. In 1968, Lawrence Robert sought out the engineers to build to advanced research projects agency's computer network- THE ARPA NET, based on special purpose mini computers called interface message processors and revolutionary technology called Packet Switching.

EMAIL

In 1972, Raymond Tomlinson invented the "Killer App" known as E-Mail. It was the first system able to send mail between users on different host connected to ARPANET. When the app wasn't there, mail could only be sent to users if they use the same computers, but this app just made everything more convienient.

WWW

In 1990, Tim Berners Lee developed a software called World Wide Web also known as WWW. It helped people to use the internet and computers. World Wide Web opened up to the public on internet in 1991. It transformed the world and made life easy.
